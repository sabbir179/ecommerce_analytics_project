# Retail Product Context

Online grocery and e-commerce journeys are sensitive to availability, delivery fees, substitution confidence, checkout speed, and mobile usability.

Mobile traffic is often high volume but lower converting because users browse quickly, face smaller screens, and may abandon when checkout requires too much effort.

Returning and loyalty customers usually convert better because they trust the retailer, have saved preferences, and understand delivery options.

Commercial impact should consider both conversion lift and average order value. A small conversion improvement can be meaningful when applied to high-volume segments.
