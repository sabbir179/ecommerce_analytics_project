# Product Analytics Glossary

Conversion rate is the share of sessions or users who complete a target action. In this project the primary conversion is payment completion per session.

Average order value is total revenue divided by completed orders. It helps size commercial impact when conversion changes.

Funnel drop-off is the share of users or sessions lost between two funnel steps. Large drops between add to basket and checkout start often indicate friction, unexpected fees, availability concerns, or poor checkout experience.

Active users are users with at least one session in the selected period.

Uplift is the difference between variant and control performance. Absolute uplift is measured in percentage points. Relative uplift divides the absolute difference by the control rate.
